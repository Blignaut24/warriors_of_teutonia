Warriors of Teutonia - Rules of War

  • Hammer: Beats Shuriken and Dragon, but loses to Wizard and Shield.
  • Shield: Beats Wizard and Hammer, but loses to Dragon and Shuriken.
  • Shuriken: Beats Dragon and Shield, but loses to Hammer and Wizard.
  • Dragon: Beats Shield and Wizard, but loses to Shuriken and Hammer.
  • Wizard: Beats Hammer and Shuriken, but loses to Dragon and Shield.
  • Confetti: When you win a round, a confetti animation will automatically start. To stop the animation and proceed to the next round, simply make your next move by clicking one of the player icons.
